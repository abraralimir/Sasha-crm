# **App Name**: SashaLeads AI

## Core Features:

- Lead Management: Centralized platform to manage leads provided by Sasha Consulting, including lead details, contact information, and status tracking.
- ROI Prediction: AI-powered prediction of potential ROI for each lead, leveraging historical data and market trends, displayed in an intuitive way.
- AI-Driven Timeline Generation: Automatic generation of project timelines with AI tools (Genkit and Gemini, using the persona of Sasha) for proposals, considering project scope and complexity.
- AI-Powered Risk Analysis: AI to conduct risk assessments of the leads and opportunities, so that users are equipped to evaluate whether or not to move forward. Analysis leverages AI tools such as Genkit and Gemini, using the persona of Sasha, acting as a 'tool' which decides whether specific risks should be brought to the users' attention.
- Secure Authentication: Sign-up and sign-in functionality, secured with modern authentication methods, with data stored securely in Firestore.
- Collaboration Tools: Integrated communication and collaboration features for Bitsteck and Sasha Consulting teams, enabling real-time discussion, document sharing, and progress updates.
- Reporting Dashboard: Customizable dashboard displaying key performance indicators (KPIs), lead conversion rates, expected revenue, and project status for comprehensive performance monitoring.
- Chat Page: Chat page where bitsteck can chat and share files and ai in the backend have knowldge of shared files
- AI Chat Section: Ai chat section where ai will have whole platform and current projects knowlegde through firebase database and talk and take smart decisions
- Enhanced Video Calling: enhanced video calling take some good open source things and video calling or normal voice calls and sharing screen for great collaboration easy scheduling and calnders

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#3F51B5) to convey trust, professionalism, and stability, reflecting the consulting nature of the application.
- Background color: Light gray (#ECEFF1), a very desaturated shade of blue, to ensure readability and a modern aesthetic.
- Accent color: Violet (#9575CD) to highlight interactive elements and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque sans-serif, is recommended for its neutral and modern appearance, suitable for both headlines and body text.
- Clean and professional icon set, consistently used throughout the application, with subtle animations to enhance user experience.
- Clean, intuitive layout with clear visual hierarchy, prioritizing key information and ensuring ease of navigation for users with same access and roles.
- Subtle transitions and animations to provide feedback and guide users through the application.
